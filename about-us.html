A creative design & technology team. We Build Great Products.

Our vision

Our goal is to build and grow qualified digital products. Our Process is based on new technologies, design-thinking approach and rapid modernising of already created product(s).
The process
  • We built our processes around quality-driven development and unit tests. Code review and QA testing are part and parcel of our standard operating procedure.
  • We follow the agile methodology to stay flexible and meet your needs as they change and grow along with your project.
  • We make sure our products run fast and are easily scalable. We also ensure that the deployment process runs smoothly with no server interruptions. At the same time, we are well aware of taking advantage of the Cloud services around the Internet.
Quality
  • From the starting of any project, a dedicated team will be available, including QA testing and lead developer mentoring. We just love to keep the code clean and maintainable and accessable to you from day one.
  • Each line of code we produce is checked by another developer from the team (peer code review).
  • Our testers verify all functionalities to spot all potential issues before you do (QA testing stage).
  • We use feature branches and Pull Requests to document our work on a specific feature.
Transparency
  • We believe Walking on water and developing software from a specification are easy if both are frozen.
  • Once a week we report the project’s progress to you and set the priorities for the next iteration.
  • You get full access to our project management system and the codebase on your selected repo preference. You can also monitor progress on a daily basis and review the latest version of the project online at any time on the staging server.
  • You can always get in touch with the project team via your project’s chat room on Slack or Skype.
Continuity
  • We have operated on the Web development market since 2014 and have been growing ever since. You can be sure we will not be going anywhere after the end of your project.
  • Thanks to our peer code review procedure, there are always a few members of our team who are familiar with your business objectives and the technology behind the project.
  • Your project’s transition to another (inhouse or outsourced) team will be smooth and easy thanks to our high-quality code.
  • We offer mentoring and coaching on the agile techniques and programing and Design Tools if you would like to follow our practices yourself.
Technology
  • Our technologies of choice are Ruby on Rails, Swift and Objective-C for iOS, Java for Android, AngularJS, Ember.js, React.js, React Native for our frontend and others.
  • We are experienced in integrating our software with a number of external services or APIs such as Salesforce, MailChimp, Google Analytics, Amazon Web Services, Social Platforms, etc.
  • We have vast experience with payment provider integration, and we have already worked with many powerful solutions, e.g. PayMill, PayPal, Skrill, PayU and Stripe.
  • We love cloud-based infrastructure and also have experience with configuring and maintaining a multi-server infrastructure, including docker-based deployments. And platforms like Amazon AWS or DigitalOcean.
Security
  • We have worked on applications for 3D Geo Mapping, banks and e-commerce. We have also developed trading systems ,large social networks and GEO Technologies for Custom 3D Mapping Projects. Those projects helped us gain sufficient experience that allows us to develop applications with state-of-the-art security measures.
  • We are your technological partner and will not leave you alone with any technological issues. We will always help you with the code, servers, integrations and domains.
  • You can always count on our support crew in case of an emergency. On top of that, we strive for our applications to have a "self-healing" system that can respond to issues by itself.
  • We design backup systems at the application level, (On top of our auto server backup systems) which will help you if anything goes wrong.
Speed
  • To get your product reaching the market quickly, we love to use the agile workflow, which allows us to deploy working software very early.
  • Our workflow is based on weekly iterations. We want you to start off each weekend by enjoying new working functionalities.
  • We put a lot of effort into producing your application’s code as fast as possible, while taking care to maintain its high quality at all times.
  • We have experience with cloud-based infrastructure such as Amazon AWS, Heroku, DigitalOcean.
Trust
  • You can terminate our relationship for any reason without any prior notice if you are unhappy with the first month of our collaboration which is a Trial Period for you.
  • The code we create belongs to you, and you have full access to it from day one.
  • We keep your business confidential, and we are happy to sign NDAs.
  • Your intellectual property is protected, and our contract guarantees that you are 100% the owner of the IP.
